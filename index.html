Theme 02

Power sources for robotic actuation

Demo video 1 · operation of the pneumatic source

Low-heat and near-silent pneumatic source

  • Integrated endothermic and exothermic chemical reactions were used to generate pneumatic power with reduced heat generation.
  • The system was designed to provide mobile pneumatic power with low acoustic noise for soft robotic applications.
  • The platform was demonstrated as a compact and quiet alternative to conventional compressed-air supply methods.
Demo video 2 · self-temperature-regulating mechanism

Electronics-free, self-temperature-regulating pneumatic source

  • An electronics-free pneumatic source was designed to maintain stable temperature while delivering portable pneumatic power.
  • The system couples hydrogen peroxide decomposition with carbon dioxide phase change to achieve self-temperature regulation.
  • The source was developed for robotic applications requiring quiet, mobile, and thermally stable pneumatic actuation.
